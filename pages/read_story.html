The Visionary

Dr. Mala kumari

Founder & Managing Director

"We didn't just want to serve food; we wanted to serve memories. My vision for Shudh India was to bring the sanctity of a home-cooked meal into the fast-paced corporate world, without compromising on quality or tradition."

With over two decades of culinary expertise, Dr. Mala kumari founded Shudh India Catering with a single pot and a thousand dreams. Today, her philosophy of 'Atithi Devo Bhava' (The Guest is God) guides every plate we serve.

2008: The Foundation

Shudh India starts as a small kitchen in Uttar Pradesh serving 50 tiffins a day. Our mission was simple: Pure, Sattvic food that nourishes the soul.

2012: The Breakthrough

We bridged the gap between traditional taste and corporate efficiency. Major multinationals trusted us to fuel their workforce with hygiene and punctuality.

2016: Innovation

Opened our state-of-the-art central kitchen. Introduced automated roti makers and eco-friendly packaging to meet growing demand.
